About Us

Our company provides a wide range of high quality professional services in the field of civil, commercial, administrative and financial law.

The Legal Department provides services that cover all legal aspects of comparative law, banking law, tax law, property rights, transport law, European Union law, international and administrative law.

Our management services department provides legal services for the formation and management of companies, works on behalf of clients with banks to provide banking services, provides operational management (mainly back office services) and provides other types of services.

The Finance Department advises on all aspects of both national and international taxation. In particular, on the financing of projects related to corporate activities, in which we offer a full range of financial planning and structured finance services.

Our company, on behalf of our clients, provides services related to the establishment of trust companies in Portugal, as well as provides services for the management of trust companies and trust funds.
